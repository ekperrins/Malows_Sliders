📐 Score Formula

Below low-end:
  score = tier_mult × (1 + urgency × 3)
  urgency = (low − value) / range

In comfort zone:
  score = tier_mult × (1 − position × 0.8)
  position = (value − low) / range

Above high-end:
  score = −(value − high)
